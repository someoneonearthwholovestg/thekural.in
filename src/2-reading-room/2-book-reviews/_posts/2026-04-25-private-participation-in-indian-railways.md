## Indian Railway

Indian Railways is one of the world's largest rail networks — a sprawling public enterprise that carries over 8 billion passengers annually and serves as the backbone of freight movement across the subcontinent. For decades it has been wholly government-owned, but the question of private participation has grown louder with each passing year.
