Welcome to my second post! If you're reading this, your blog is working perfectly. 🎉

Jekyll makes it incredibly easy to manage content. Here’s a quick checklist of what you’ve set up so far:

- ✅ `_config.yml` for site settings
- ✅ `_includes/` for reusable header & footer
- ✅ `_layouts/` for page templates
- ✅ `_posts/` for your articles

## Why Markdown is Powerful
You don’t need to write HTML for every post. Just use:
- **Bold** for emphasis
- `inline code` for commands
- Lists for steps
