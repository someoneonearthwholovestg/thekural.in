## A Letter from Titan

### Drifting Beneath Orange Skies

**Dear Earth,**

*I never imagined silence could feel this alive.*

> The winds here do not howl—they whisper in methane and memory.

From the surface of Titan, everything feels distant, even time itself. The sky glows in a hazy orange, and the lakes shimmer not with water, but with liquid hydrocarbons. It’s strange, beautiful… and a little unsettling.
